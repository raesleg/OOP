- inheritance: --|>
- implementation: ..|>
- composition: *--  (filled diamond; whole creates and owns part lifecycle)
- aggregation: o--  (hollow diamond; whole holds reference to independently-existing part)
- association: -->
- dependency: ..>

      as stubs in an "External" package. Only extends/implements arrows to
      external types are kept. Field (-->) and dependency (..>) arrows to
      external types are omitted for diagram clarity.
